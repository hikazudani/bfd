The first trip we planned was our own.

Ever since, we’ve been working to make travel better for everyone.

Here’s how it all started…

We started as two friends exhausted from the corporate routine who decided to test if a few intentionally planned days could truly change how we see life. We studied climates, less-obvious routes, hidden cafes, and discreet trails. Each experimental trip generated notes, adjustments, and a principle: traveling isn’t about accumulating places, it’s about creating internal space. See our methodology and understand why we design journeys with meaning at every step.

Today, we apply this learning so you don’t have to spend hours comparing blogs. Our curation combines rest, local culture, nature, and small optional challenges. We offer clear logistics, best travel times, sustainable alternatives, and real human support—no meaningless automated responses. Instead of just “escaping,” we want you to have moments that truly make sense: breathing fresh air, slowing down, rediscovering curiosities, and returning inspired.

We want you to come home with more clarity, energy, and simple stories that make you smile. If you're ready to transform the way you use your days off, we're ready to plan together. Shall we begin?

Our Features

We Monitor the Weather

Download our app and we’ll warn you if it’s about to rain in the next 20 minutes near your location. A good downpour can be refreshing, but sometimes you just want to stay dry.

Global Guides

We’ve explored the planet for the best retreats and incredible views. If there's a corner you want to escape to, we know the most inspiring places.

Wi-Fi Hotspots

We only recommend trips to places we can guarantee are amazing. We map out local Wi-Fi hotspots and mark them all on the app's map.

Survival Kit

Every time you book an escape with us, we send you a survival kit with essential supplies. It helps you set up a tent, start a fire, and (almost) solve anything.
